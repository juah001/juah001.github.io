Our safety and security

Our procedure

  1. Client makes reservation
  2. Autohost syncs with our property management software
  3. Preliminary analysis and risk profile created
  4. Client completes the online digital check-in portal
  5. Autohost screening AI runs full verification assessment, flagging issues and concerns
  6. Verified guest receives their check-in instructions for a seamless experience

Advanced guest Verification: Using AI technology, we rigorously vet guests through ID verification, criminal background screens, credit assessments, and sex offender searches. Business rules instantly alert our team to flagged profiles, ensuring only reputable guests stay.

Booking Assurance: Every guest provides a security deposit and agrees to our digital contract, detailing all house and community rules. Non-compliance results in fines and immediate removal. Chargeback protection further guarantees transaction security.

Preventative Security measures

Minut detector: Tracks noise, temperature, and occupancy. Real-time alerts help maintain a peaceful environment and protect your property’s integrity.

Ring Doorbell: Live video monitoring of entrances ensures only verified guests enter. Clear footage and real-time alerts allow swift response to concerns.

Smart Lock System: Each guest receives a unique check-in code. Codes reset every stay, delivering a seamless, keyless experience with high security.
